Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SiteSureLabs and AI-powered construction inspection.

What types of defects can SiteSureLabs detect?

SiteSureLabs can detect a wide range of construction defects including cracks, moisture damage, misalignment, surface irregularities, and structural anomalies. Our AI model is continuously trained on real-world construction data to improve accuracy.

How accurate is the AI analysis?

Our AI achieves high accuracy by leveraging deep learning models trained on thousands of annotated construction images. Results include confidence scores and severity ratings so your team can prioritize effectively.

Can I use SiteSureLabs with a live camera feed?

Yes! Our Live Inspection feature lets you stream directly from a camera and see defect analysis in real time. It's ideal for on-site walkthroughs and progress checks.

What image formats are supported?

SiteSureLabs supports all common image formats including JPEG, PNG, and WebP. Simply drag and drop your site photos into the Image Analysis dashboard to get started.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All uploads are processed securely and are not shared with third parties. Your construction data stays private and is used only for generating your inspection results.

Can I export inspection reports?

Yes. After analysis, you can export structured reports with defect locations, severity levels, and AI-generated summaries — perfect for documentation, compliance, and team sharing.
